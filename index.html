DAILY MIND & BODY CHECK-IN

**Private:** Your goals. Your rules. **Start Monday!**

1. Weekly Flow

M/W/F **Strength Routine** (15-20 min) - Private time only.
T/Th Active Rest: Stretch, Walk, or **Playing Tag**.
Sat **Cardio:** 4-5km walk/run or long game of Tag.
Sun **Full Rest** (Exercise). **Flexible Meal Day** (Nutrition).

2. The 15-Minute Routine (M/W/F)

Complete 2 Sets of each exercise. **Rest 30 seconds between Sets.**

1. Bodyweight Squats: 10-12 reps
2. Incline Push-ups: 8-10 reps (Use a desk, chair, or wall).
3. Plank Hold: 30-45 seconds
4. Reverse Lunges: 8 reps per leg (Step backward and bend both knees).
5. Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds (Fast core move, run knees to chest).

4. Stealth Workout Tips

  • **The 5-Minute Rule:** If you feel lazy, only commit to the first 5 minutes. You can stop after that.
  • **Push-up Disguise:** Do your **Incline Push-ups** against a wall when no one is looking.
  • **Plank/Lunges:** Best done silently on your bedroom floor when you have privacy.
  • **Squats:** Do 10 deep squats when you "drop" something or are bending to look at the bottom shelf of a cupboard.
